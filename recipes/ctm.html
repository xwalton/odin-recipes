Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

I want you to stop and think for a moment about the best chicken you've ever had. And I do mean the best! What was it like? Juicy? Tender? Flavorful? All of the above? Dream no more! This recipe will give you the best chicken you've ever had.

    Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Tumeric
  • Cayenne
  • Cinnamon
  • Black pepper
  • Cardamom
  • Tomato sauce
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter

    Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Grill the chicken
  4. Finish the sauce
  5. Combine the chicken and sauce
  6. Enjoy!
